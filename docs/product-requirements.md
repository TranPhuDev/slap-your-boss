# Product Requirements

**Slap Your Boss** is a 15-second cartoon stress-relief game.

Flow:

```text
Landing with suited character
-> enter boss name
-> choose/capture photo
-> local face detection and preview
-> PLAY
-> 3, 2, 1, SLAP!
-> tap/swipe for 15 seconds
-> Slap Report
-> replay/new boss
```

Required metrics:

- Boss name
- Total Slaps
- Max Combo
- Face Damage
- Stress Released
- Best Slap
- Final Score
- Rank

Ranks:

- 0–29 Calm Employee
- 30–49 Office Rookie
- 50–69 Deadline Fighter
- 70–89 Corporate Survivor
- 90–100 Boss Battle Legend

Constraints:

- no login/backend/database/online leaderboard;
- no server photo storage;
- no real-time AI image generation;
- cartoon comedy, no realistic injury.

Privacy copy:

```text
Your photo is processed only on this device and is never uploaded.
```

Safety copy:

```text
Did your boss just scold you?
```
